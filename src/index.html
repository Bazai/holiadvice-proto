Why Holiadvice

Holiadvice is an International internet portal which functions as a complementary and integrative medicine therapy center via the Internet. Through the portal, anyone can apply to a practitioner anywhere in the world and obtain treatment without coming to him physically, but via the Internet.

Via Holiadvice, simultaneously, complementary and integrative practitioners or other practitioners (whose treatment does not require physical contact while treating), can treat people from all over the world despite not being located nearby.
